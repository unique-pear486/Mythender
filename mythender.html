Weapons Quick Reference

When using a weapon, you can either charge one column (check the top box) and take no bonus dice or might, or drain all the charged columns (check the bottom box) to get those bonuses.

Intrinsic Weapons keep their non-drained charges after a battle for free.

Bonuses from Relic Weapons may become temporary Thunder for free.

Companion Weapons charge when you are Harmed.

Mythic Forms & Fate Quick Reference

Whenever you use the Mythic Die, you must check off a Mythic Form box, starting with the top-left and proceeding left-to-right and down. If both boxes in a row are filled, you now appear as that Form.

After rolling the Mythic Die, consult the number next to the box you just checked. If the die is equal or higher, you've lost a piece of your humanity. Congratulations! Check the next Fate box and take a new Gift either now, or at a time when it's neccesary.

At the end of the battle, if you checked off a Fate box that has a number on it, you'll check to see if you've Fallen Into Myth.
